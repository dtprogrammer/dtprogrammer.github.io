Question

Say you have an array for which the ith element is the price of a given stock on day i.

Design an algorithm to find the maximum profit. You may complete as many transactions as you like (ie, buy one and sell one share of the stock multiple times). However, you may not engage in multiple transactions at the same time (ie, you must sell the stock before you buy again).

Solution

  • The problem is very similar to 121. Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock with the only difference that we can buy and sell as many times as we want but can't hold more than one stock at a time.
  • So basically we want to buy and sell as many times as we can while making a profit to obtain maximum profit. It's our greedy approach.
  • It should be noted that buying a stock at lowest price and holding it not making transaction to sell at peak price does not yield a better result which can be seen in the graphs below.
